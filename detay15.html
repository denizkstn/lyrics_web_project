Kashtan Annotation: Drake rekindled his relationship with his father after years of estrangement around 2013, here he describes a relationship where they now get intoxicated together every night and talk. Drake receives advice from his father to be cautious around women, warning him that the woman make like Drake for his fortune and fame but ultimately do not care about him. Even though Drake does not think this advice is sound, he still nods and agrees with his father. Drake has previously described his father’s advice as less than helpful, like in 4PM in Calabasas when he says “Mike never tried to rap like Pac, Pac never tried to sing like Mike” Those my dad’s words to me when I asked him how to make it in life And I always said my mother gave the greatest advice, yeah
