Kashtan Annotation: An “itchy trigger finger” is when the finger of a person is very eager to fire their weapon or likely to do so unexpectedly. Following the previous lines anticipating a new era, Doja Cat uses this metaphor to show that she’s back in the game and ready to attack. She then makes a wordplay with the word “gift,” as in a present that you can “wrap up” and give to someone else, and a natural ability or talent that continues to drive her career, which is nowhere near ending or “wrapping up.” inally, “ripped a new ribbon” is very likely a reference to the Creation myth, in which God takes a ribbon from Adam and uses it to give life to Eve, showing that Doja is similarly giving birth to a new herself.
