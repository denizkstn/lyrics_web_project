Kashtan Annotation: Drake bragged about the women in his contacts list in similar fashion on “First Person Shooter” from his 2023 album For All The Dogs where he rapped: I search one name, and end up seein' twenty tings Nadine, Christine, Justine, Kathleen, Charlene, Pauline, Claudine Man, I pack ‘em in this phone like some sardines Just as it did then, this may interpolate the iconic second verse of “What These Bitches Want” by DMX from his 1999 classic album …And Then There Was X. In the verse, DMX lists out his romantic history by first names: There was Brenda, Latisha, Linda, Felicia Dawn, LeShaun, Ines and Alicia Teresa, Monica, Sharon, Nicki Lisa, Veronica, Karen, Vicky This may also be an interpolation of the 2007 track “Shawty Is Da Shit” by The-Dream where featured artist Fabolous similarly asks: Is it Keisha? Is it Tisha? Maybe Lisa? Or Teresa? It could be Tia, maybe Aaliyah “Is it PARTY?” jokingly refers to the album’s other featured artist PARTYNEXTDOOR.
