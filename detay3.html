Kashtan Annotation: In Caribbean dancing, “to wine” refers to the movement of your hips in a circular motion. It is derived from the word “winding”. “Six-side” refers to the city of Toronto. The city is famously known as “The 6”, a nickname popularized by Drake. The name comes from the city’s six original municipalities and its area codes, 416 and 647. “Yute” is Jamaican slang for youth or young people.
