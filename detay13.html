Kashtan Annotation: Travis is calling out the girl for hiding her wild side but also pointing out that this girl isn’t into the usual flashy stuff like vacations and champagne. She’s not chasing luxury or trying to impress anyone, which makes her different from the crowd. That lowkey, down-to-earth vibe is probably what draws him to her. Instead of the soft life, she’s about going all in. “Going hard in the paint” is a phrase from basketball that means giving something your full effort, no holding back. So she’s raw, real, and not afraid to get aggressive when it comes to getting what she wants. This line is also a reference to Waka Flocka’s song “Hard in the paint”.
