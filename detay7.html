Kashtan Annotation: This line is interpolated from a Central Cee’s 2024 COLORS SHOW performance of “One By One”: If it weren’t the UK, would’ve had an AK If it was America, I would’ve had a Draco Cench also used these lines on his 2021 unreleased freestyle “Meme Page”: If it weren’t the UK, would of had a AK But we got spinners and baby nines The UK has strict gun control laws, making it difficult to acquire firearms like assault rifles. In a place with easier access to guns, he would have had high-powered weapons, like an AK-47 with extended magazines (“a hundred rounds”). The UK law requires a firearms license and background checks conducted by local police before anyone can legally own a gun. The country’s heavily monitored borders make it difficult to smuggle firearms into the UK. As a result, most guns in circulation are old and previously owned, often making them unreliable. Acquiring a powerful and widely recognized rifle like an AK-47 is nearly impossible, as major producers like Russia, the United States, and China face significant barriers when attempting to supply such weapons to the UK.
